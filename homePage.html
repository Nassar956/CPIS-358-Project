Problem Statement

Food allergies are a life-threatening problem. Hidden allergens and confusing labels make grocery shopping dangerous. Current apps often fail to detect hidden derivatives.

Our Solution

Allergic Food Scanner provides an accurate detection system for both visible and hidden allergens, giving you peace of mind.

Key Features

  • Hidden Allergen Detection: Finds derivatives other apps miss.
  • Risk Reporting: Green (Safe) vs Red (Risky).
  • Profile Management: Customize your specific allergies.
